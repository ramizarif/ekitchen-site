How it works

From saved recipe to cooked meal

eKitchen connects every step between finding a recipe and putting food on the table.

Find something new

Browse recipes by cuisine, difficulty, and cook time. Import recipes from any website, TikTok, or Instagram. They all land in your cookbook, ready to go.

Know your kitchen

Tell eKitchen what's in your fridge and pantry. It doesn't have to be exact. It shows you what you can cook right now and what's only an ingredient or two away.

Shop with a plan

Pick one recipe or pick five. eKitchen checks what you need against what you have and builds a shopping list with prices that fit your budget.

Cook step by step

Step-by-step cooking mode walks you through each recipe. When you're done, rate it, leave a tip, and track what you've cooked.

Built for people who want to cook more

If you're stuck making the same meals every week and you've got a growing pile of saved recipes you never get around to, eKitchen was built for you.

It's not another recipe box. It's the system that ties your kitchen together. Know what you have, figure out what you need, and actually cook something new tonight.
